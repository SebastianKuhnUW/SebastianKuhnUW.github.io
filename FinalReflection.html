Final Reflection

This page tries to summarize my insights from TECHIN 521 under a couple of key insights

First of all, this course has been a really fun ride. After this class, I feel much more comfortable working with ambiguity because I know that ambiguity does always exist, but it can be handled with the right process at hand. There two largest take-aways from this class for me are the following:

Insight #1 - I am amazed at how much we created in these 10 weeks we were working together. I attribute this to operating under a clear project structure and time constraints. In future creative work, I would want to keep this style of work going and establish frequent deliverables to hold project members accountable and mentally involved.

Insight #2 - We never lost focus of the problem we were trying to solve and the path was always clear. I think this is because we were following the design question as our true north. For future creative work, I will always want to establish a mutually understood and adaptable design question every project member knows.
